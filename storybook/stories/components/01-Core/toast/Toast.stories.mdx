# Toast

Toasts are non-modal, time-based window elements used to display short messages; they usually appear at the bottom-center of the screen and recommend they disappear after 5000 milliseconds.

They use a fixed width, and their height depends on the length of the notification message. Limit toast notifications to two lines of text.

The default version should be used on light backgrounds, while you can use the light versions on dark backgrounds.

# Notifications

Notifications are messages that communicate information to the user. Use notifications to inform users of updates or changes to system status. Sharing information with users and providing immediate feedback is essential for building trust. While notifications are an effective method of communicating with users, they are disruptive and best used sparingly.

Notifications are available three different types: _Success_, _Error_ and _Informational_ and are made up of the following:

- **Icon**: Informs users of the kind of notification at a glance.
- **Alert Title**: Gives users a quick overview of the notification.
- **CTA Link (optional)**: Link that allows users to address the notification or navigates them to a page with further details.
- **Close button**: Closes the notification.
- **Message**: Provides additional detail and actionable steps for the user to take.
